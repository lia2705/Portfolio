Corvus Sanctum

a VR escape room

Role: Map design, 3d modeling, Poster design

Tools: Unity, Blender, Miro, Canva

Team: Group Project

Corvus Sanctum is an immersive VR escape room that puts players in a tense, atmospheric experience. Awakening in a coffin, they must solve a series of challenging puzzles to escape the grasp of a mysterious doctor. Every action matters — making too much noise could alert the doctor and jeopardize their escape. The game combines problem-solving, stealth, and immersive VR design to create a thrilling adventure.
